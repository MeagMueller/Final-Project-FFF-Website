Vinca

Final Form Fusion Director

My name is Vinca! I've been dancing since 2004, teaching since 2014, and gaming for longer than I can remember. I love performing to intense, emotional pieces, and my favorite prop to dance with is a fan veil. Thanks to my cosplay addiction, you can often find me performing in homemade garb, and almost always wearing one of my many wigs. â€‹ I love performing to video game music because of the stories that lie behind them. It's about more than just the movements; it's about the narrative I'm connecting with, and the emotions I'm trying to share with the audience. We're all familiar with Link, but what about the bosses? How would they perform their battle-dances, what would they emphasize, how would they feel, and why? Telling stories is what inspires my dance, and wanting to share those stories with others is why I perform.
